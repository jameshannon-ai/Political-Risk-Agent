# Showcase Cases

## Strait of Hormuz Transit Controls: Shipping Operator Exposure

The flagship maritime showcase applies the reusable Political Risk Agent to a UK shipping-operator voyage decision around Hormuz-linked transit controls. It focuses on transit, delay, reroute or legal/compliance escalation decisions, with source-audited coverage of transit controls, sanctions red flags, insurance repricing, vessel-flow signals and route-cost trade-offs.

Files:
- `hormuz_shipping_operator_brief.md`
- `hormuz_source_audit.md`
- `hormuz_evidence_pack.json`

Value:
- The brief gives operator decision stance, quantified evidence, voyage decision matrix, sanctions escalation logic, dynamic route-cost assessment and relaxation tests.
- The source audit shows research plan, source hierarchy, selected/rejected source reasoning and quantified source coverage.
- The evidence pack preserves machine-readable source requirements, extracted claims, scores, quantified facts and score bridge.

- `sanctions_source_audit.md`
- `sanctions_evidence_pack.json`

Value:
