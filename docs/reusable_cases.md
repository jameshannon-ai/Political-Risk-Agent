# Reusable Political Risk Cases

## A. Sanctions Exposure For Trade Finance Lenders

- Topic: New sanctions affecting commodity exports from a high-risk jurisdiction
- Business user: `trade_finance_lender`
- Source categories: official sanctions notices, bank compliance updates, company disclosures, reputable news, specialist sanctions analysis
- Risk drivers: sanctions exposure, counterparty risk, payment disruption, cargo documentation, collateral enforceability
- Output focus: transaction screening priorities, escalation triggers, documentation checks and collateral risk controls

## B. Election Risk For Investors

- Topic: Election uncertainty affecting regulated infrastructure investors
- Business user: `consultant`
- Source categories: election commission updates, polling data, market indicators, reputable news, specialist political analysis
- Risk drivers: policy reversal, regulatory intervention, currency volatility, contract sanctity, institutional stability
- Output focus: scenario outlook, portfolio exposure, decision triggers and policy-watch indicators

## C. Civil Unrest Risk For Supply-Chain Operators

- Topic: Civil unrest disrupting port access and inland transport
- Business user: `importer_exporter`
- Source categories: official security notices, logistics company updates, port authority notices, reputable news, specialist security analysis
- Risk drivers: transport disruption, supplier shutdown, port access, inventory pressure, security risk
- Output focus: route alternatives, supplier exposure, inventory actions and customer communication priorities

## D. Regulatory Change Risk For Insurers

- Topic: New insurance regulation changing claims handling or capital requirements
- Business user: `marine_insurer`
- Source categories: regulator notices, industry association updates, company statements, market indicators, legal analysis
- Risk drivers: policy wording, capital adequacy, claims handling, compliance deadlines, pricing adequacy
- Output focus: compliance actions, wording review, capital impact, operational readiness and board reporting

## E. Conflict Escalation Risk For Shipping Operators

- Topic: Regional conflict escalation affecting a strategic maritime route
- Business user: `shipping_operator`
- Source categories: official maritime advisories, carrier updates, port authority notices, vessel-flow data, reputable news, specialist maritime analysis
- Risk drivers: vessel security, route disruption, crew welfare, port access, fuel and insurance costs
- Output focus: routing decisions, crew safety, customer commitments, cost impact and operational watchpoints
